El video Tutorial: Phonemic awareness.

This video presents the English teacher Erick Ceciliano Matamoros doing a demonstration of phonemic awareness lesson for second grade. Representing all stages of pedagogical mediation starting with the pre-teaching (Warm up, Activation of Prior Knowledge, Modeling, Clarifying). Teachers can follow a sequence of a task: pre-task, task rehearsal, task completion and task assessment using different resources. In the task of Mini-project, there are some ideas where learners can integrate learnings and linguistic skills.

Transforming the English Classroom through Action –Oriented Teaching and Learning

This tutorial video presents the English teacher Jonathan Elizondo sharing a successful experience with first grade called Mini Books: A suitable task for Mini Projects. In the new English Curriculum at the end of each unit, there is a task where learners create a Mini Project to reinforce and demonstrate the learning acquired during each unit. Also he shows three different examples of Mini-Books for the Units All about me, Going to School, So Cool!, and My neighborhood.
